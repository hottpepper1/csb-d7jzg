The Affinity Collective

Pixel Pixys wants to grow and support upcoming women and girls in the nft space. We know there are tons of talented female artists out there that may be lacking the resources to enter the space. We will do our part to lift them and provide financial, educational or other assistance as needed for contract launches, team building or any areas they need help with.
10% of all primary sales will be reinvested in upcoming female artists!

Objectives

  • Empowering female creators through education, financial or other assistance
  • Promoting and Showcasing these phenomenal artists
  • Awards will be community designated and granted by the Affinity Collective
  • Taking in consideration the recommendations of all Pixy NFTs owners

Causes we support

At Pixel Pixys we recognize mental health/substance abuse awareness and support is now more important than ever. It’s only fitting that we would support these causes in the real world. 3% of all primary sales will be donated to each of these 3 causes:

NAMI

NAMI is the nation's largest grassroots mental health organization dedicated to building better lives for the millions of Americans affected by mental illness.

Shatterproof

Shatterproof is a national nonprofit organization dedicated to ending the addiction crisis in the United States.

World Federation for Mental Health

The mission of the World Federation for Mental Health is to promote the advancement of mental health awareness, prevention of mental disorders, advocacy, and best practice recovery focused interventions worldwide.

CREATOR AND ARTIST

The whole collection is the work of Jo, a self-taught innovative and fearless creator and artist in many mediums both tangible and digital. She is a firm believer that anyone can do anything they want to if they put their heart into it.

As the creator of Pixel Pixys, she believes that there are many talented female artists unable to enter the nft space, either due to a lack of resources or a lack of means. It is her goal to assist in providing these resources to those in need and provide them with the support they need.

THE AFFINITY COLLECTIVE

It will operate freely but will be guided by the suggestions and recommendations of the Collective community. A specific channel will be opened on discord where submissions can be entered and reviewed and another for 'voting polls' where we will ask the Collective community's opinion on selecting the awarded recipients.

It will vary greatly depending on the artists needs. To try and support as many as possible, We will look for talented artists with great potential and aim to help them cross financial or development hurdles with contract creation or anything else they may need and that may stand in the way of them entering the scene.

The Affinity Collective will make all of it's artist visible and will help to promote them, here, as well as on Twitter and Discord.
